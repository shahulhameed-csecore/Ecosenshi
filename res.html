About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to reforesting the planet and preserving biodiversity. Our work spans continents, helping to bring life back to deforested areas and providing habitat for wildlife.

Our Projects

Amazon Rainforest

Restoring critical forest cover in the Amazon basin to support indigenous communities and wildlife.

African Savannah

Working with local communities to plant native trees that combat desertification and restore ecosystems.

Asia Pacific

Implementing agroforestry projects that provide sustainable livelihoods and protect watersheds.
